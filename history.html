Our history and tradition
throughout the years!

In the heart of Dublin's vibrant culinary scene, a cherished old hamburger recipe from 1960s is set to make waves, drawing inspiration from an unlikely source.

The story of Burger.Island begins half a century ago in a small Italian village nestled in the rolling hills of Tuscany. The grandmother, Nonna Lucia, was renowned throughout the region for her culinary prowess, particularly her secret hamburger recipe. In an era where traditional Italian cuisine reigned supreme, Nonna Lucia's burgers stood out as a unique and delicious departure from the norm. Made with the finest locally sourced beef, hand-ground and seasoned with a blend of aromatic Italian herbs and spices, Nonna Lucia's burgers were a labor of love.

Each hamburger ingredient is carefully formed by hand, grilled to perfection, and served on a freshly baked ciabatta bun, creating a culinary masterpiece that captivated the palates of all who tried it. With each bite, customers were transported to the hills of Tuscany, where the air is filled with the scent of olive groves.
Burger.Island quickly became a beloved fixture in the Dublin food scene, praised for its commitment to quality ingredients, authentic flavors, and warm hospitality.
